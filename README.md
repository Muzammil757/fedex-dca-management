## Problem Statement
Managing overdue customer accounts through external Debt Collection Agencies (DCAs) is currently manual,Time taking, fragmented, and opaque. The existing process relies on spreadsheets, emails, and manual follow-ups, leading to delayed recoveries, weak governance, and limited visibility.The process is not trustworthy too.

## Proposed Solution

A centralized, AI-enabled DCA Management Platform that replaces manual, spreadsheet-driven debt recovery with a structured, transparent, and scalable digital workflow.

The solution demonstrated through the live website provides:
 1.Centralized case lifecycle management from allocation to closure
 2. AI-driven case prioritization and recovery risk indicators
 3. SLA-based workflow tracking with escalation visibility
 4.Real-time dashboards for recovery, risk, and performance
 5.Structured collaboration and accountability for DCAs
 6.Comparative DCA performance and SLA adherence insights
 7. Audit-ready tracking for governance and compliance
 8.Modular, future-ready design for AI, automation, and RPA integration

This approach improves recovery efficiency, predictability, and operational control at enterprise scale.
